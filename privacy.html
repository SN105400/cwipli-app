Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026.

Cwipli ("the app") is a personal automation tool that publishes short gaming highlight clips to the account owner's own connected social accounts (TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube).

The app accesses only the permissions the account owner explicitly grants via OAuth, used solely to publish the owner's own content. We do not collect, store, sell, or share the personal data of any third party. Access tokens are stored securely on the owner's private server and are used only to publish the owner's own clips. The owner may revoke access at any time in their platform settings. No data is processed for advertising; the app reads only each platform's native post metrics for the owner's own posts.

Contact: the account owner via their connected accounts.
